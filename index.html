About

Jen is a full-stack developer and one-time English major based in Nashville, TN, who has been delighted to discover that she loves crafting words together in ways that will do her bidding just as much in software languages as in human ones. While historically a big fan of sitting down inside and developing opinions on film and literature, Tennessee has helped her realize that she actually enjoys doing things that require getting up and moving around sometimes, too. So in her free time… much to teenage Jen’s shock and awe… you may now also find her hitting up the yoga studio, camping with friends, or out hiking one of her favorite trails around town.
